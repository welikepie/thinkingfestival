Workshops

Usability Testing

Usability Testing How-To's (For Small Teams & Little Budgets) with Evgenia Grinblo.

A full-day workshop taught by UX Specialist Evgenia Grinblo. Usability testing is a crucial part of the process of designing digital products but is often over-looked due to time & money constraints. With Evgenia, you can learn how to test a products usability without great expense.

Games Design

Games Design and Iteration at High Speed with James Wallis

Learning how to work with paper prototypes to iterate the project quickly can save you days. In this workshop James Wallis will teach you to create and flesh out a concept for a simple multi-user system, build a working prototype, watch as other people test it and critique it, and use that criticism to rework the design towards a finished product. All with a gamey twist, you'll be conceptualising and building your own game!

Design Modelling

Design Modelling with Jude Pullen

We've teamed up with Sugru's chief engineer, Jude Pullen, to do a half-day 'design modelling' workshop. One of Judes' passions is cardboard modelling; working from the sketch up to build a prototype out of cardboard. Prototyping is a great way to communicate an idea and in this workshop you'll learn to turn your ideas into final working protypes.
