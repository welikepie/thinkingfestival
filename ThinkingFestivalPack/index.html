Thinking Festival - Sponsor Pack

Thinking Festival is a four-day celebration of User Experience.

With a conference, a barcamp, workshops and a variety of fringe events; it’s going to be an exciting event attracting up to 500 interaction designers, information architects and user experience designers from all over the globe.

Thinking Festival Events

Thinking Festival will be home to a whole host of exciting and engaging User Experience events, with a variety of opportunities to learn.

  • Thinking UX - A full day, one-track conference containing talks from experienced professionals, catering to 350 people passionate about UX.
  • UXCamp London - A barcamp for 500 eager industry professionals who want to demonstrate their knowledge. All talks are proposed on the day by anyone in attendance who wants to talk, discuss or share.
  • UX Workshops - Full day and half day workshops from talented UX folk where you can learn important and useful skills.
  • Fringe Events - Other activities to keep you busy between the conference and the barcamp. A walking tour of London, museum tours and coffee tasting workshops.
Thinking UX - Conference

Thinking UX is a brand new community conference where talented professionals will take part in this one-day one-track conference to share their ideas and experiences. All with an aim to leave each attendee with useful insights and skills related to UX. We expect 350 attendees.

UX Camp London

Unlike the traditional conference, UX Camp London is an event where the content of the day is shaped by its attendees.

Using the BarCamp format; every attendee has a chance to hold a session. At the start of the day there is a schedule where time-slots can be booked in rooms to give a talk and this is entirely first-come, first-served.

The goal of UXCamp is to share knowledge and ignite discussion. We’re expecting 500 Industry Professionals to attend.

Fringe Events

Throughout the festival, we're working on a whole host of other activities that our attendees can get involved with.

  • A tour of the interesting agencies and start-ups in London
  • A Ghost Walk of London, taking a tour of the city and hearing spooky tales associated with the area
  • A tour of the Victoria & Albert Museum
  • A coffee tasting workshop
Why Sponsor Us?

If you become a sponsor of Thinking Festival or any of the events within it, your brand will be visible in front of a potential 500 industry professionals over the course of the festival, where you’ll be viewed as a supporter of the event.

You'll also be featured widely accross our online presences. The UX Camp London website has received 6000 visits in the past 6 months and the Event Handler website has been viewed 20,000 times over the course of this year. Not only that, but your logo has the potential to feature in our Event Handler email campaigns that are sent to over 1800 subscribers each week, as well is all campaigns related to Thinking Festival.

Sponsoring the Thinking Festival is a fantastic networking opportunity with a potential to engage with new users, employees and clients.
