Sjors TimmerDesigner

Sjors Timmer is a freelance Interaction Designer interested in exploring and stretching the boundaries of design. He has worked for the Ministry of Justice, for agencies and start-ups.He is actively involved in the design community, where he co-organised UXcamp London and UXDO, a series of practical workshops for designers. He has given a number of presentations on a broad range of design subjects for diverse audiences of professionals, including a presentation on designing with time in mind at EuroIA and the design process of Rem Koolhaas at LondonIA. He has written for well-known blogs and has been featured in .Net magazine. He holds an MA in Digital Media from Goldsmiths, University of London.

Anne DitmeyerEditor

* Maker of maps

Anne S. Ditmeyer is a designer, editor and consultant with a focus on travel and design. She started Prêt à Voyager in 2007 as a creative way to explore the intersection of travel and design with the motto "travel is not about where you go, but how you see the world".

Her online presence has opened many doors including offering Map Making and InDesign classes on Skillshare, giving tours of Paris through Vayable, working as a long-time contributing editor for Design*Sponge, teaching a Designer/Entrepreneur class at Parsons Paris, and traveling to cool places on assignment, with the overall goal of helping to connect creatives across continents.

Her latest endeavor is Studio/Practice, a curated library of tips and tools for creative business, where she is co-founder and editorial director. You can find Anne on Twitter and Instagram as @pretavoyager.
